### **My Fitness Goals**

1️⃣ **Train 4 times per week** – Maintain a sustainable and balanced workout routine without overloading my body.

2️⃣ **Focus on upper body strength** – Build well-defined shoulders, back, arms, and chest.

3️⃣ **Maintain lower body health** – Keep my legs strong while avoiding excessive strain on my knees and lower back.

4️⃣ **Core and stability** – Strengthen my core muscles for better posture, injury prevention, and overall body control.

5️⃣ **Safe and effective exercises** – Prioritise machine-based exercises and avoid movements that could worsen knee or back issues.

6️⃣ **Balanced training structure:**

- **Warm-up**: Light cardio + joint mobility exercises
- **Strength training**: Upper or lower body focus
- **Post-workout cardio**: For fat loss and endurance (optional)
- **Stretching**: To improve mobility and recovery

7️⃣ **Consistency over intensity** – Avoid unnecessary strain and focus on long-term progress.

8️⃣ **Improve flexibility and mobility** – Include stretching and mobility exercises to feel better in daily life.
